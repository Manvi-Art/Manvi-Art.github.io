About the Artist

Hello ! Welcome to my artistic world !

My name is Manvi C., and I’m a Contemporary Artist who specializes in the mesmerizing world of fluid art. Fluid art is a contemporary style of abstract painting that involves the skillful manipulation of various liquid mediums. I use different techniques like pouring, tilting, swirling and blowing to craft dynamic and unpredictable patterns, as well as stunning color combinations.

A canvas is a playground for my imagination, where I bring my thoughts and emotions to life. In every stroke and hue, I want to convey the essence of life through my creative expression. Join me on my artistic adventure and delve into the multitude of my creations where each art piece tells its own unique story.

Manvi C.
